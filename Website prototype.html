    About Us

The Avocado Pit

Helping Chicago Live a Healthier Life

Locally sourced from the surrounding Midwest, we will be able to provide freshly farmed ingredients without charging a premium price for the food. At The Avocado Pit, we don’t just purchase ingredients, we work with farmers by offering them a stake in the company to ensure that the customers food is their passion. When it comes to combining the ingredients however, we’ve consulted world-renowned chefs in order to create menu items that will change the perception that healthy can’t mean tasty. With food this good, it will be hard to know it’s vegetarian. This is why The Avocado Pit will be popular not just among vegans and vegetarians but among your everyday Windy City residents. At The Avocado Pit, we cater to Chicago’s busy, bustling lifestyle by providing healthy food in a timely manner and at an affordable cost.

Mission

    Our mission is to provide quality vegan and vegetarian food at a quick pace for an affordable price.

Vision

    We envision helping Chicago live a better lifestyle while changing preconceptions about vegetarian cuisine.

Tasty, Affordable & Timely: Your Health Is Our Passion

Values

  • Quality Assurance: From our food to our customer service we listen, consider and react in order to ensure that The Avocado Pit is synonymous with quality.
  • Health: We are dedicated to embracing healthy active lifestyles whether an employee, customer or neighbor.
  • Environmental Protection: We locally source as much as we can in order to fight pollution caused by trucking and overseas shipments.
  • Time Efficiency: At The Avocado Pit we realize that our customers time is just as important as ours. Knowing this, we pledge to serve our food as fast as possible.
  • Respect: Between our customers, employees and the Earth itself we value respect above all else. Most importantly we respect the difference of ideas and welcome meat eaters and non-meat eaters alike to the table.

Our team is thoroughly pleased to introduce you to The Avocado Pit and we look forward to showing you the further progress we make on the restaurant. We see a fast-service vegetarian- friendly restaurant as the best fit to introduce into our chosen Chicago location. We value health, environmental protection, time efficiency, quality assurance, and respect here at our restaurant. We hope to provide quality and affordable vegetarian food in a timely manner to the Chicago community. Don't hesitate to give us a call at 773-555-9796.
